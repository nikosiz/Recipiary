Moist Gluten Free Pumpkin Muffins

Make your favorite fall treat easier with this moist gluten-free pumpkin muffins recipe that is packed with flavor and easy to prepare!

PREP TIME

30min

SERVINGS

12 servings

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Line muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  3. In a large bowl mix together melted butter, coconut sugar and brown sugar.
  4. Add eggs and mix to incorporate.
  5. Add pumpkin and vanilla extra and mix until just combined.
  6. Add flour, spices, baking soda and salt. Whisk together until incorporated.
  7. Evenly divide batter into 12 baking cups.
  8. Bake for 25 minutes or until tooth pick or skewer comes out clean.

Ingredients

2 cups gluten free flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
⅛ teaspoon salt
2 teaspoon cinnamon
½ teaspoon nutmeg
½ teaspoon ground ginger
½ cup unsalted butter, melted
¾ cup coconut sugar
¼ cup brown sugar
2 eggs
⅔ cup pumpkin puree
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
