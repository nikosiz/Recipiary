Easy Mini Chicken Pot Pies

Follow this easy to make mini chicken pot pies recipe that is ideal for serving to the family on busy weeknights! A delicious comfort food meal everyone loves.

PREP TIME

1h 10min

SERVINGS

6 servings

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Place 4-6 ramekins inside a larger baking dish or baking sheet. This prevents spills in the oven and makes it easier to transfer.
  3. In a Dutch oven or heavy-bottom saucepan, heat the olive oil, onions, celery, and bell peppers over medium-high heat. Cook for 3-4 minutes or until tender.
  4. Stir in the diced chicken, flour, and seasonings and stir to combine then cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  5. Add water, heavy cream, and the peas and carrots then bring to a boil, stirring constantly to prevent burning.
  6. Reduce heat to medium and continue cooking for 10-15 minutes until the mixture is very thick.
  7. Remove from heat, and divide the mixture between the ramekins.
  8. Cut the puff pastry into circles the size of the ramekins and place them on top of the filling mixture.
  9. Brush the tops of the pastry with melted butter.
  10. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown on top.

Ingredients

1 tablespoon olive oil
2.5 cup rotisserie chicken, diced
¼ cup onion, diced
¼ cup celery, diced
¼ cup bell pepper, diced
½ teaspoon garlic powder
½ teaspoon onion powder
½ teaspoon Italian seasoning
1 tablespoon chicken bouillon
¼ cup all-purpose flour
1 cup water
½ cup heavy cream
8 oz. frozen peas and carrots
1 puff pastry crust
1 tablespoon butter, melted
