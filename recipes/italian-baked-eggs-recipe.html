Italian Baked Eggs in Tomato Sauce

These Baked Italian Eggs will make your next brunch exciting and super delicious! These look amazing plus are super easy to make! Serve them with some toasted bread, and you have the perfect breakfast!

PREP TIME

25min

SERVINGS

4 servings

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425˚F or 220˚C.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the eggs.
  3. In a saucepan, heat oil on medium settings. Add in the onion and sweat the onion for 1 minute.
  4. Add in the garlic and sauté for an additional 30 seconds.
  5. Add the tomatoes, spices and half cup of water. Leave on medium heat for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Take off the stove. In a casserole, add the tomato sauce.
  7. Top off with cheddar cheese.
  8. Crack three eggs into this casserole and bake in the oven for 10 minutes, or until the egg whites are completely cooked through.
  9. Serve hot with toasted bread.

Ingredients

3 eggs
3 medium tomatoes, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic
½ medium onion, finely chopped
1 tablespoon thyme
1 teaspoon red chilli flakes
½ teaspoon red chilli powder
½ teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cumin
¼ cup sharp cheddar cheese
½ cup water
2 tablespoon cooking oil
parsley, for garnishing
