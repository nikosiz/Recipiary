Crispy Baked Salt and Pepper Chicken Wings

You are going to fall in love with this easy and delicious recipe for baked salt and pepper chicken wings. A delicious appetizer and fun chicken recipe everyone loves!

PREP TIME

45min

SERVINGS

5 servings

Instructions

  1. Pat chicken wings dry using paper towels. Make sure that they are as dry as possible. If you have any extra time, leave wings to dry out in the fridge for around an hour, uncovered.
  2. Preheat the oven to 210C/410F. Line baking sheet with foil, or use parchment.
  3. Mix salt, garam masala, baking powder and sugar in a large bowl. Add chicken wings into a bowl, drizzle olive oil on top and mix everything, making sure to cover the wings evenly.
  4. Layer wings on a baking sheet, making sure they are not touching.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  6. Serve with lime wedges, topped with chopped scallions.
