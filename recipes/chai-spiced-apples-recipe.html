Chai-Spiced Baked Apples with Vanilla Bean Gelato

For a decadent fruit course, your family will never forget, turn to these chai-spiced baked apples with gelato. Permeated with the fragrant spices of chai tea—warm cinnamon, spicy ginger, and pungent clove—this comforting dessert will fill your house with aromatics and your belly with bliss.

PREP TIME

1h

SERVINGS

1 serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400° and set out a 9×13-inch baking dish, 2-quart casserole dish, or pie dish.
  2. Peel, core, and quarter the apples. Slice them into even-sized lengthwise pieces about 1-inch thick and place them into the dish.
  3. Add the lemon juice, butter, apple cider, vanilla, brown sugar, cinnamon, cardamom, ginger, cloves, nutmeg, black pepper, cornstarch, and salt to the apples. Toss to combine and then loosely cover the dish with foil.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes, uncover the dish, toss the apples, and continue baking until caramelized and fork-tender, about 10-15 more minutes.
  5. Allow the apples to cool slightly and then serve with even portions of the vanilla bean gelato.

Ingredients

4 medium apples (2 sweet like honey-crisp and 2 tart like granny smith)
1 tablespoon lemon juice
2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
2 tablespoons apple cider (or apple juice)
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
6 tablespoons dark brown sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon ground cardamom
1 teaspoon ground ginger
¼ teaspoon ground cloves
¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
2 tablespoons cornstarch
pinch coarse salt
1-pint vanilla bean gelato
