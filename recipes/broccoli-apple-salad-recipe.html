Sweet and Savory Broccoli Apple Salad

A favorite side dish at barbecue events, potlucks, and parties has always been this easy sweet and savory broccoli apple salad recipe. It seems like it might be a bit too much, but really, the ingredients complement each other beautifully. Combining a simple homemade poppy seed dressing with broccoli, onion, berries, nuts, and apples creates a sweet and savory side dish that is full of fiber, protein, and nutrients!

PREP TIME

15min

SERVINGS

8 servings

Instructions

  1. Prepare your fruit and vegetables to assemble. Wash all vegetables and pat dry as needed.
  2. Start by trimming the stems from the broccoli and cutting the crown into small florets around 1" to 1 ½" in size.
  3. Peel and chop the onion.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, add the broccoli, blueberries, dried cranberries, red onion, walnuts, and sunflower seeds. Stir to combine and distribute ingredients evenly.
  5. Core and chop the apple(s) into 1" pieces then place into a medium bowl and add toss with 1 tablespoon lemon juice then set aside.
  6. In a medium bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, mayonnaise, honey, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, apple cider vinegar, and poppy seeds until combined.
  7. To the broccoli mixture, add the apples then the dressing.
  8. Stir well until all pieces are coated.
  9. Refrigerate until time to serve.

Ingredients

1 ½ lb fresh broccoli florets
1 cup fresh blueberries
¼ cup chopped red onion
½ cup dried cranberries
¼ cup sunflower seeds
½ cup walnuts
1 green apple, chopped
1 red apple, chopped
1 tablespoon lemon juice
½ cup Greek yogurt
1 cup mayonnaise
¼ cup honey
1 tablespoon lemon juice
2 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
1 tablespoon poppy seeds
