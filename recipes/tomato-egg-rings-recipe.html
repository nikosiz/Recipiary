Easy Tomato Egg Rings with Herbs

A simple tomato egg rings recipe is packed with flavor and protein for a great breakfast that is ready in minutes!

PREP TIME

2min

SERVINGS

1 serving

Instructions

  1. Slice a large tomato into ½" thick rings. Use a knife or a glass to cut the inside out of the tomato rings and set the rings aside, then dice the tomato that you cut out of the center, into small pieces.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the eggs.
  3. Add in the tomatoes, fresh chopped parsley, salt, black pepper, and the all-purpose flour until it is combined well.
  4. In a large non-stick skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  5. Place the tomato rings into the hot skillet, then fill each well with the egg mixture.
  6. Let cook for one minute, then flip and continue cooking for an additional 30 seconds to 1 minute until the egg is cooked through.
  7. Remove from the pan and serve hot.

Ingredients

1 large tomato
2 large eggs
1 tablespoon fresh parsley
¼ teaspoon salt
¼ teaspoon black pepper
1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
