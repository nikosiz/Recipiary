Homemade Butter Cookies

This simple homemade butter cookies recipe is a perfect rich treat that is easy to make, perfect to tuck into lunchboxes and ideal for cookie exchanges!

PREP TIME

25min

SERVINGS

24 servings

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 320°F/160°C and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, beat the softened butter and sugar together until creamy, approximately 2 to 3 minutes on medium speed.
  3. Add in the milk, flour, almond flour, and salt and stir until a dough forms.
  4. If making different colors, divide the dough into three equal portions. Add green food coloring to one and cocoa powder to the second. Leave one plain.
  5. Prepare three piping bags with a star-shaped tip on each then put the different colors of dough into the three different bags.
  6. Pipe the dough onto the prepared baking sheet as star shapes until all the dough is used.
  7. Bake for 15 minutes then let stand on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to finish cooling.

Ingredients

⅔ cup unsalted butter, softened
⅔ cups powdered sugar
8 tablespoons milk
1 ¼ cup all-purpose flour
7 tablespoons almond flour
½ teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon cocoa
green food colour
