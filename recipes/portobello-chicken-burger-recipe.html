Portobello Mushroom Chicken Burger

A delicious portobello mushroom chicken burger is a healthy and easy meal with tons of flavor! The perfect spin on a Caprese burger for your summer garden parties!

PREP TIME

25min

SERVINGS

2 servings

Instructions

  1. Wipe the mushrooms clean and remove the stems. Then cut slits along the top of the mushroom.
  2. In a grill pan or large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon olive oil and the garlic cloves over medium heat.
  3. Once the garlic is aromatic, add the mushrooms to the pan and sprinkle with the rosemary, thyme, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes per side, drizzling additional oil over the top once turned. Cook until browned and softened but still holding shape nicely.
  5. Set aside the mushrooms, and place the chicken breasts into the same pan.
  6. Lightly season the chicken with the remaining salt, black pepper, and a bit of chili powder if desired.
  7. Cook the chicken for 3 to 4 minutes per side, until golden brown and an internal temperature of 165°F, has been reached. Remove from the pan.
  8. To assemble the burger, place a mushroom top side down, then top with a chicken breast.
  9. Add cheese and tomato on top of the chicken.
  10. Then top with the second mushroom cap as a bun.

Ingredients

4 portobello mushrooms
2 garlic cloves
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1 teaspoon dried rosemary
2 thin sliced chicken breasts
2-4 slices mozzarella cheese
1 tomato, sliced
1 teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon black pepper
¼ teaspoon chili powder
2 tablespoons olive oil
